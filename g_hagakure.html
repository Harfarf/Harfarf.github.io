Developed during the 2022 Global Game Jam

'Hagakure' is a puzzle game in which the player is immersed in the life of a young oriental girl who seeks to come to terms with the duality in her soul: calm and impulsiveness, good and evil, calm and destruction, ...

Will our young Shinkei manage to discover the beauty in her?
