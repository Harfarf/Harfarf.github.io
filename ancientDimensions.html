Ancient Dimensions is a first person shooter with a portal based combat system and cell shading art style.
You'll take on the skin of an explorer doctor and, together with your assistant robot Ed-U, you'll have to travel to different dimensions through portals to investigate the mysterious energy that the dimension stones hide.
